$f(x) = -x^{3} + 8x^{2} - 5x + 1$<br>
<br>
The x-coordinate of $P$ is 4.<br>
$f'(x) = -3x^{2} + 16x - 5$<br>
$f'(4) = -3\cdot 16 + 16\cdot 4 - 5 = 11$<br>
$y = 11x + b$<br>
<br>
The x-coordinate of $P$ is $4$, so the y-coordinate is:<br>
$f(4) = -64 + 8\cdot 16 - 5\cdot 4 + 1 = 45$<br>
$45 = 11\cdot 4 + b$<br>
So $b = 1$<br>
The tangent line is: $y = 11x + 1$<br>
